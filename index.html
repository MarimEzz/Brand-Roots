Who Are We

What Do We Do

We Brand Roots are a service-oriented company established in 2016 with its objective and main purpose to offer registration services for other companies who are willing to register fertilizers or pesticides to be able to trade them in the agriculture market in Egypt. Registrations are necessary to legally sell and trade any Fertilizer or Agricultural a Pesticide in the Agricultural Field. For any company to import a Fertilizer or Agricultural Pesticide from abroad or even locally manufacture a product, it is necessary to go through the registration process first.

We started by specializing in procedures that concern Fertilizer and Agricultural Pesticide Registrations. Over time, we also started offering other registration services for other products like Public health pesticides, Feed additives & Pet animal foods, Antiseptics & disinfectants. We also help our clients in finding new foreign companies who sell fertilizers and pesticides so they can register and buy their products and sell them in the Egyptian market, and be their distributors or their agent in Egypt.

Our Clients

The Way We Work

This is our main field “REGISTRATIONS”. Our clients are companies that sell fertilizers and pesticides in the Egyptian market. Their experience is specialized in marketing these products to reach the highest sale targets and widest spread of their product’s range using their technical and commercial experience. This makes them busy and very dedicated to the market so they usually outsource a company like ours to finish all processes related to the Ministry of Agriculture like “REGISTRATIONS”. We guarantee that we are BEST at our field “REGISTRATIONS” and hence “IMPORTING FERTILIZERS”.

Registrations are a very complicated process that takes months and our other goal is to also make the largest number of registrations possible, registered under our name, ready to use at any time. Therefore, at any time a local company wants to import a product, it will be ready registered in Egypt to be imported any time without having to wait for the registration process to be completed. This will speed up opening new co-operations between manufacturers of Fertilizers and Pesticides worldwide with importers of these products here in Egypt who are our main clients.
